Introduction to SubCleaner


Do you own a DivX Hardware Player?

If the answer is yes, you are maybe in my situation. I'm the owner of a LinkTheater. This device works well but it can be very picky with subtitles file. One wrong character, one empty line and boom, the player stops the subtitle in the middle of a movie!

The solution: SubCleaner

This application can:
• remove unnecessary characters & formatting errors
• limit the number of chars per line & the number of lines per subtitle
• convert the following subtitle formats to SubRip format (.srt):
    - MicroDVD
    - SubViewer
    - SubViewer 2.0
    - TMPlayer

Just drag a subtitle on the application and it will be automatically cleaned.
